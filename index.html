About Us

Shaping the Future of Flexible Packaging

Established in 1976, Al Sharq Flexible Packaging has grown to become a regional leader in film and pouch solutions for food, pharma, and industrial sectors.

Driven by innovation and quality, we serve clients across the Middle East and globally with high-performance packaging materials backed by world-class manufacturing.
