About Al Sharq

From Humble Beginnings in 1976 to Industry Leadership Today

Who We Are

Pioneers in Flexible Packaging Since 1976

Founded by Mr. Ali bin Suleiman Al Shehri, Al Sharq Flexible Packaging began as "Al Sharq Factory for Plastic and Paper". From a single blown film plant in Riyadh, we’ve grown into Saudi Arabia’s largest plastic producer.

With 6 fully integrated factories and over 300 skilled professionals, Al Sharq is recognized for delivering advanced extrusion, printing, and lamination solutions across global markets.

  • Over 4 decades of innovation and excellence
  • ISO 22000:2005 and global safety standards
  • Trusted in 4 continents across multiple industries
